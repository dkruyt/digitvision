Welcome to the Neural Network App! Here’s how to use it:

  • Input Layer: Draw on the grid by clicking cells to toggle between black and white. This represents your input to the neural network.
  • Predict: Click "Predict" to see what the neural network thinks the digit is based on your input.
  • Clear: Use "Clear" to reset the input grid.
  • Prepare Data: Click "Prepare Data" to load training data. This data is used to train the neural network.
  • Train: Train the neural network by clicking "Train 1 Epoch", "Train 10 Epochs", or "Train 100 Epochs". Training improves the network's accuracy.
  • Clear Model Data: Use this button to reset the neural network, clearing all trained data.
  • Hidden and Output Layers: These show the activations of neurons in the hidden and output layers. Clicking on output neurons will train the network with your input as the corresponding digit.
